Mantra Labs built an AI-enabled data platform that empowered Pace Industries to achieve 99.9% pricing accuracy and 4x faster insights, leading to optimized inventory management and a 60%+ decrease in data processing errors.

Scenario

Pace Industries faced challenges with data trapped in silos (Excel, ERP) and a reliance on manual processes for pricing updates, leading to slow and error-prone reporting.

The complex customer-specific pricing calculations and high volume of daily transactions further exacerbated these challenges, hindering efficient analysis and decision-making.

Solution

Mantra Labs built a unified data platform leveraging Azure Data Factory and Azure Synapse Analytics, breaking down data silos and automating data processing for real-time insights.

The platform enabled automated report generation and real-time price modeling, empowering Pace Industries with faster, more accurate business decisions and improved operational efficiency.
